About Raquelita's

Raquelita's came to be as all great ideas do, while surrounded by family at the dinner table. Owner and chef Raquel Cuesta-Pena wanted to create a space to share her homestyle cooking with the rest of the world and so Raquelita's was born. Raquelita's serves a variety of food and drink options for the whole family. We serve high quality food that will be sure to leave you wanting more. Stop in today and we promise you won't be disappointed!
